About Us

SPAKS LUBE Private Limited

Established in 2006, Spaks Lube Private Limited has grown to become a prominent leader in the manufacturing of sealants, coatings, and emulsion paints. Originally started as a proprietorship, the company has recently transitioned into a private limited entity, underscoring its growth, modernization, and commitment to robust corporate governance. With over a decade of excellence, Spaks Lube has solidified its reputation as a trusted brand, delivering innovative and high-quality products to meet diverse industrial and consumer needs.

OUR PRODUCTS

SPAKS's high quality products deliver performance & beauty.

Emulsion Paints:

Known for their smooth finish, vibrant colors, and durability, our emulsion paints are a top choice for homeowners and commercial projects alike. They are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions while maintaining their aesthetic value.

Interior products

Waterproofing Solutions:

One of our flagship innovations is our patented waterproofing product. This technology is designed to protect porous substrates such as concrete, brick, masonry, high-pressure pipelines, and overhead water tanks from water absorption. It exemplifies our commitment to solving real-world problems through cutting-edge research and development.
